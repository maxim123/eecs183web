Teams

If you have a team for the final project, register your team by 11:59 p.m. on Monday 11/3.
Once you register your team, team members may not change, but you may choose
to work on a different project before submitting the proposal.

If you do not have a team for the final project, please fill out a form to be entered
into the system for automatic partner matching. The deadline for automatic
partner matching sign up is 11:59 p.m. on Tuesday 11/4. Filling out this form
is the only way to ensure to be matched with a team. As the number of students
wanting to work on a project might not be evenly divisible by four,
no guarantees that your project preference will be gratified.
